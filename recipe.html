Chicken Penne

1 tbsp oil
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1 inch cubes
1 lb dried penne pasta
1 yellow onion, thinly slices
2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1/2 cup pesto sauce
1 cup frozen peas

Directions

In a Dutch oven or soup pot, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and season it with salt and pepper to taste. Cook, stirring often, until it becomes golden, about 6 to 8 minutes. Once the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165Â°F, remove it from pan and set aside. In the same pot, add the onion and cook, stirring, until it becomes soft and translucent, about 2 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
